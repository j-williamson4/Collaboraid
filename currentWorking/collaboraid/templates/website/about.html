Let this famous person persuade you

CollaborAid, as you might guess, stems from the words "collaborate" and "aid". Our founder Hugh Jackman believes strongly that a person's greatest accomplishments are those that benefit their community. He started CollaborAid to bring the people of Central Texas together and generate exponential good. At many benefits, this handsome fellow uses his rugged good looks to bring in those dollars.

The folks behind CollaborAid know that bringing together in volunteerism can change lives. Our volunteers are awesome. It's simple. They work hard and generate outcomes. The outcomes can be long-lasting and life-changing.
